Glossary

Wins - The number of wins with the given civ.

Losses - The number of losses with the given civ.

Win% - The percentage of games the given civ won.

First Pick - The number of times the given civ was picked first. Note first picks are hidden from the players and could be mirrored.

Pick - The number of times the given civ was picked.

Ban - The number of times the given civ was banned.

Pass - The number of times the given civ was not picked and not banned.

First Pick% - Given that the civ was not banned, the percentage of civ drafts where the given civ was picked first.

Pick% - Given that the civ was not banned, the percentage of civ drafts where the given civ was picked.

Ban% - The percentage of civ drafts where the given civ was banned.

Pass% - Given that the civ was not banned, the percentage of civ drafts where the given civ was not picked.

Played% - The percentage of times when the given civ was played when the given civ was picked. (Wins + Losses)/(First Pick + Pick)
